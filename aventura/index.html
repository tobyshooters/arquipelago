PIBESP (Projeto de Instalação de Bancos no Estado de São Paulo) is a collective effort, a mutirão, to expand seating options in public spaces in the city of São Paulo. “O Rosinha” is our first intervention, located in Praça General Craveiro, in front of Artacho Jurado’s Edifico Viadutos.

BALNEÁRIO SENTÔ

Balneário Sentô was part of Rafael's Architecture final graduation project in FAU-USP. Constructed as an assemblage of industrial materials, this unconventional mobile bathing space was constructed in Rafael's home in São Paulo. Now it is in the care and private collection of artist Fernanda de Barros Carvalho.

CONVIVIAL FORMS

An object serves as an index of the social relations operating around it. A pair of CF3 chairs were installaed at Forsyth Plaza, across Luis’ fruit staill. From there on, they gained autonomy. Each Convivial Form is accompanied by an instruction manual. They are open-source. Wood, saw, and screws are all that is needed.
